# Who is IMC?

# Introduction

IMC is a global research-driven quantitative trading firm. Since 1989, we’ve been a stabilizing force in financial markets, providing essential liquidity upon which market participants depend. Across our offices in the US, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, our quant researchers, traders, software engineers, and business operations professionals are united by our uniquely collaborative, high-performance culture, and our commitment to giving back. From entering dynamic new markets to embracing disruptive technologies, and from developing an innovative research environment to diversifying our trading strategies, we dare to continuously innovate and collaborate to succeed.

# Locations

We have 10 global offices with >1900 employees, covering all time zones and 90+ trading venues. Our offices are in Aarhus, Amsterdam, Chicago, Hong Kong, London, Mumbai, New York, Seoul, Sydney, and Zug.
