### What is the Tutorial Round?

The Tutorial Round is available to teams immediately after signing up until Round 1 begins. You will receiving crucial information about the platform interface, on-board advisor selection, and the opportunity to engage in a practice algorithmic trading round. You can test your Python program as many times as you like and receive instant feedback on its performance. Make the most of your time in the simulator, because once you land on Intara, the time to practice is over and your real mission begins.

### What is the signup deadline?

Your signup process must be completed before Round 1 begins on at 12:00 CEST (UTC+02:00) on April 14, 2026.

### Can I participate if my university is not listed in the drop-down list during the sign-up process?

Yes, no problem. We try to keep a comprehensive list, but new players are joining from every corner of the globe. Simply select “Other” and continue on. This will not impact your ability to participate in Prosperity. Validation and other eligibility criteria will only be requested when the final leaderboard reveals the Top 25 teams. You may email [prosperity@imc.com](mailto:prosperity@imc.com) to request your university be added for future editions of Prosperity. 

### How can I connect with fellow players?

You can join our dedicated Prosperity Discord server.

### How do I write a trading program?

There is elaborate documentation on the [Writing an Algorithm in Python](writing-an-algorithm-in-python.md) page. Check it out to see how to write a program. Still have questions? Our [Discord](https://discord.gg/SABeB8uKxd) server is a good place to ask questions.

### What is the schedule for each round?

The round schedule is [Round Schedule](round-schedule.md) .

### What is an On-Board advisor?

This is an advisor your team captain selects before Round 1 starts. The Team Captain may change the advisor during the Tutorial Round, but will be locked when Round 1 begins. If one has not been selected, one will be assigned to your team. Each of the 3 advisors have their own unique personality but are guaranteed to be great guides and offer words of wisdom for the toughest moments throughout the challenges. They offer the same information, just in different communication styles. Suit the advisor that suits you best! 

### What do I do with the badges I earn?

Whatever you like! Some are just for fun and others are to reward top performance. Show off your hard work and progress within Discord or LinkedIn! You can always check in on what you’ve earned and what’s still locked under Crew Honours in your menu.

# **Eligibility & Team Formation**

### Can I change teams?

In your Team Settings, you can accept or reject invitations from other teams. This is possible through the end of Round 2. After this, your team formation is locked. 

### Are multinational teams allowed?

Yes. Team captains will select a single country that best represents their team, but this is for leaderboard filtering by country. Be mindful of the eligibility to win criteria when forming multinational teams.

### Are teams from different universities allowed? 

Absolutely! 

### When is team formation locked?

Team formation is locked when Round 2 closes.

### Can I rename my team after it’s created?

Yes, the team captain can.

### What is the responsibility of team captains?

They name the team, may add or remove team members, and select the on-board advisor. Everyone on the team has equal access to the challenges.

# **Challenge Submissions & Logistics**

### Does a new round open immediately after a previous round is closed?

There is a calculation period after the round closes. Please refer to the [Round Schedule](round-schedule.md) .

### The platform shows that it is “calculating scores”. When will the next round open?

It takes roughly 3 hours between rounds to calculate scores. You will be notified via email and in the Discord chat when this phase is over.

### If I submitted a file before the round ends, is it going to be processed?

If this file was not marked as “active” in the UI, then your file was not submitted in time. If you had a previously submitted a file marked “active”, this will be counted as you final submission and your round scoring will be based on this. 

### Do Manual Challenges have an impact on my Algorithmic Challenge PnL?

No, they are independent of each other. You can think of manual challenges as a nice way to earn a bit of extra profit and learn new skills.
